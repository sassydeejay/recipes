Spinach Nuggets

2 10oz. pkg frozen choped spinach
1 tbls. minced dried onion
2 cups herb-seasoned stuffing croutons
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
2 beaten eggs
3 tbls melted butter or margarine

In saucepan cook the spinach and dried onion according to spinach package directions, drain well.  In mixing bowl combine spinach mixture croutons and cheese, stir in eggs and melted butter.  Shape into 1 inch balls.  Place in shallow pan, cover and freeze.  Transfer to freezer.  At serving time place frozen nuggets in a shallow baking pan and bake at 375 for 15 or 20 minutes or until heated trhough.  Makes about 48.

If you skip freezing step, bake a few minutes less.
